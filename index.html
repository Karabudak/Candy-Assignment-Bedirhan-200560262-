Interesting Facts

Smarties comes in 6 colors/flavors:
White = Orange/Cream
Pink = Cherry
Green = Strawberry
Purple = Grape
Orange = Orange!
Yellow = Pineapple

In Canada, Smarties candy is known as Rockets.

Smarties® are made 24 hours a day in two candy factories located in Union, New Jersey and Newmarket, Ontario. The company produces BILLIONS of Smarties® rolls each year.

Did you know the Smarties Candy Company is run by three women? After founder Edward Dee left the business, his two sons Michael and Jonathan took over the family business, and then Dee's three granddaughters, all in their 30s as of 2019, took over as co-presidents. Sarah Dee, Liz Dee, and Jessica Sawyer grew up in the Smarties factory, they absolutely intend to keep the family tradition going, hoping their children will someday take over and and truly keeping it a family tradition.

Each of the six flavors are made individually with their separate coloring and flavoring, and once compressed they're put into buckets. Each of the buckets are eventually combined to mix the colors, before they head to different machines for packaging.

Nutritional Information

Smarties only contain 25 calories per roll? Did you know as of 2019, Smarties Candy Company was producing over two billion Smarties rolls every year, according to CNBC. That adds up to around 30 billion tiny Smarties tablets. To keep up with that number, Smarties needs to operate 24 hours a day, five days a week between the both factories. That’s basically 1,000 tablets per minute!!

Did you know Smarties are peanut-free and top allergen_free? All Smarties products are free from peanuts, tree nuts (e.g. almonds, walnuts, pecans), shellfish, fish, milk, eggs, soybean and wheat. If the UPC number on the packaging begins with “0 11206”, it is manufactured in a peanut-free facility as well.

All Smarties products are vegetarian and vegan.

Do you know what the five ingredients are to make Smarties? Dextrose, Citric Acid, Calcium Stearate, Natural and Artificial Flavor, Colors (Red 40 Lake, Yellow 5 Lake, Yellow 6 Lake, Blue 2 Lake).
